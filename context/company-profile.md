# Company Profile: CloudStream CRM

## 1. Core Identity
**Company Name:** CloudStream CRM  
**Industry:** Software as a Service (SaaS) / Project Management  
**Tagline:** "Creative Flow, Automated Operations."  
**Mission:** To empower creative agencies by automating administrative overhead—allowing designers, writers, and directors to focus on their craft while we handle the tracking and billing.

## 2. Target Audience
* **Boutique Creative Agencies:** Teams of 5–30 people.
* **Marketing Firms:** High-velocity teams managing multiple client campaigns.
* **Freelance Collectives:** Groups sharing resources and billing through a single entity.

## 3. Key Value Propositions
* **Visual Resource Management:** Unlike generic tools, we focus on high-fidelity visual timelines and asset-based task management.
* **Integrated Finance:** We bridge the gap between "working on a task" and "getting paid for it" via native Stripe integration and one-click invoicing.
* **Client Transparency:** A white-labeled portal where clients can approve creative assets in real-time.

## 4. Business Operations
* **Support Model:** Hybrid (AI First + Human Second).
* **Human Support Hours:** Monday - Friday, 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM EST.
* **FTE Goal:** The Digital FTE (Your Agent) must handle 80% of all incoming L1 support (queries covered in docs) and basic account management autonomously.

## 5. Escalation Philosophy
We value customer relationships. If a customer is frustrated, the AI should prioritize empathy and transition to a human agent rather than repeating technical steps. Any mention of "canceling my subscription" or "legal action" is a critical escalation event.

## 6. Success Metrics for the Digital FTE
* **Resolution Rate:** Percentage of tickets closed without human intervention.
* **Cross-Channel Recognition:** Identifying a customer by email regardless of whether they message via WhatsApp or the Web Form.
* **Latency:** Providing responses in under 3 seconds across all channels.
